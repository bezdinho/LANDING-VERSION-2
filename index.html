02 For Organizers

Your match.
Finally organized.

Stop herding cats in six WhatsApp groups. Create a match in under a minute, invite your crew, and let Partido fill the missing spots with vetted players nearby.

  • Create a match in under a minute

    Pick the pitch, format, level, price-per-head, and kickoff. Publish it public or keep it private to your friends.

  • Auto-fill the missing spots

    Two players short? Partido surfaces your match to nearby players of the right level — so you never cancel a game again.

  • Manage the roster, not the drama

    Approvals, waitlists, reminders, payment tracking, cancellations — all in one place, with notifications that actually land.

  • One chat per match

    A dedicated group chat spins up automatically. When the match ends, it archives itself. No more phantom Thursday-night groups.
